## Content Format Guidelines

### Each Article Should Include:
1. **Introduction** (150-200 words) - Clear problem statement
2. **Main Content** (1500-2500 words) - Detailed, actionable information
3. **Key Requirements Checklist** - Bullet points of essentials
4. **Timeline/Cost Breakdown** - Visual tables where applicable
5. **Common Mistakes to Avoid** - Practical warnings
6. **Next Steps** - Clear call-to-action
7. **FAQ Section** - 5-8 common questions
8. **Related Articles** - Internal linking to 3-5 related guides

### Visual Elements:
- Process flow diagrams
- Document checklists
- Cost comparison tables
- Timeline infographics
- Location maps
- Before/after requirement comparisons

### Tone:
- **Educational** - Not salesy
- **Professional** - Law firm credibility
- **Accessible** - Avoid excessive legal jargon
- **Helpful** - Anticipate reader questions
- **Current** - Update dates and regulations regularly
